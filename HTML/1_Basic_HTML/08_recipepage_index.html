Coconut Cookies (Soft & Chewy)

Today we’re going to make a simple and delicious batch of coconut cookies. These cookies are soft on the inside, lightly crisp on the edges, and packed with the rich flavor of shredded coconut. The recipe is beginner-friendly, requires basic ingredients, and comes together quickly — perfect for enjoying with coffee, sharing with friends, or satisfying a sweet craving.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • ¾ cup (170 g) unsalted butter, softened
  • ½ cup brown sugar
  • ½ cup granulated sugar
  • 1 large egg (room temperature)
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 ¾ cups shredded or desiccated coconut

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Line two baking sheets with parchment.
  2. In a bowl, whisk together the flour, salt, and baking soda.
  3. In another bowl, beat the softened butter with both sugars until light and fluffy.
  4. Add the egg and vanilla; mix until smooth.
  5. Add the dry ingredients to the wet mixture. Stir.
  6. Fold in the shredded coconut until evenly distributed.
  7. Scoop tablespoon-sized dough balls onto the baking sheet.
  8. Bake 10–12 minutes, until edges are lightly golden.
  9. Cool on the tray for a few minutes, then transfer to a wire rack.
